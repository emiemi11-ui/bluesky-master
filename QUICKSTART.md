## ğŸ® How to Play (30 seconds tutorial)

### Basic Controls

1. **Select a Unit**
   - Click on any BLUE unit (friendly forces)
   - Unit details appear on the right panel

2. **Give Orders**
   - Click **MOVE** button
   - Click on the map where you want the unit to go
   - Unit will start moving!

3. **Attack Enemy**
   - Select a friendly unit
   - Click **ATTACK** button
   - Click near RED enemy units
   - Your unit will engage automatically when in range

4. **Win the Mission**
   - Capture all 3 objectives (hills marked with circles)
   - Stand units inside the objective circles
   - When you control all 3, you win!

## â“ Common Questions

**Q: My units won't move!**
A: Make sure you clicked MOVE button first, THEN click on the map.

**Q: Why are my units retreating?**
A: Their morale dropped too low. Pull them back and let them recover.

**Q: How do I attack?**
A: Just move units near enemies. They auto-attack when in range!

**Q: What do the colors mean?**
- BLUE = Your units (friendly)
- RED = Enemy units
- GREEN health bar = Unit is healthy
- RED health bar = Unit is badly damaged
